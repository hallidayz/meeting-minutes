# AI Guardian Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 2026

AI Guardian is a privacy-first desktop meeting assistant. Your data stays on your device.

## Data Collection

- Meeting audio, transcripts, and summaries are stored locally in SQLite and meeting folders.
- Optional anonymous analytics may be collected if you opt in (no transcript content).
- PIN-based encryption protects sensitive fields at rest using AES-256-GCM.

## What We Do Not Do

- We do not upload your meetings to our servers by default.
- We do not require cloud accounts for core functionality.
- We do not store your PIN — it exists only in memory while unlocked.

## Third-Party Services

If you configure external LLM providers (Ollama local, OpenAI, Claude, etc.), requests are sent only to endpoints you choose.
